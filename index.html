About the Data

The data showcased in this dashboard is sourced from TMDB with explicit permission for its use. The dataset consisted of all movies of all languages from 1960 to sept 2023. I started with a comprehensive dataset, underwent an extensive Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA), and after rigorous cleaning and refinement, narrowed it down to a more manageable size, focusing exclusively on English movies. The insights presented are derived from meticulous calculations and analysis, providing an in-depth understanding of movie moods and trends over the years.
